Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 11, 2023

Welcome to ValidMe, a mobile application dedicated to user identity validation. At ValidMe, we are committed to the protection of your privacy and personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

ValidMe uses the camera of your device exclusively for the purpose of identity validation. We do not request, collect, or store your location or any other sensitive information that is not strictly necessary for identity validation.

Use of Cryptographic Keys

To ensure the security and privacy of your identity:

  • We generate cryptographic keys (public and private keys) directly on your device.
  • The private key is generated and stored only on your device and is never shared or transmitted outside of it.
  • We do not keep copies or records of your private key.

Personal Data Processing

  • We do not make copies or store personal user information outside the device.
  • We only use the data necessary for identity validation, and this data is processed exclusively for that purpose.

Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not share any personal information with third parties, except as strictly necessary to comply with applicable laws or the requirements of competent authorities.

GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union. This includes ensuring your rights such as access to your personal data, its rectification, deletion, and the portability of the same.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy. Any changes will be communicated promptly through the application or by email.
